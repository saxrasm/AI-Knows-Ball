# Example: genz-detector

## Example 1 — Corporate trying to be Gen Z
**Input:**
```
/genz-detector "Hey fam! Our new product is totally bussin and on fleek! YOLO, grab yours today! We slay 💯"
```
**Output:**
```
[Authenticity Score]: 2/10
[Generation Vibes]: Corporate-trying-to-be-cool

[Slang Breakdown]:
  ✅ Correct usage: bussin (used correctly, though forced)
  ⚠️  Outdated: on fleek (2014 era, expired), YOLO (2012 era, deceased)
  ❌ Misused: "we slay" (brands don't slay, people slay), 💯 (boomer energy)

[Verdict]: Major L. This reads like a marketing intern's first draft after googling "Gen Z words 2019"
[How to improve]: Drop the YOLO and on fleek immediately. Replace with: "not gonna lie this is bussin fr, grab it before it's gone 🔥"
```
